Levels

We are a group of students from RVCE who designed this website to improve the library management system at Humanity NGO.
We have designed this website with the following features:
1)Sign in as a Volunteer
2)Add new students
3)Assign new books to students based on their age and level
4)Volunteers can custom make a test for evaluation of the students' learning.
5)Correct student answers using NLP model.
6)Keep track of student progress using a leaderboard and database. 7)Send email notifications to the volunteer if the student has exceeded the return due date of the book.
8)Inverse recommendation of books of different genres.
